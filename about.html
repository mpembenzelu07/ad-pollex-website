Our Story

Founded in 2016, Ad Pollex began with a simple mission: to help Tanzanian businesses thrive in the digital age. What started as a small team of passionate marketers has grown into a full-service digital marketing agency serving clients across Tanzania and beyond.

Over the years, we've helped hundreds of businesses transform their digital presence, increase sales, and build strong brands. Our deep understanding of the Tanzanian market combined with global best practices allows us to deliver exceptional results for our clients.
