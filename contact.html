Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with Ad Pollex.

What is your typical response time for new inquiries?

We strive to respond to all new inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly at +255 684 320 325 for immediate assistance.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer a free 30-minute initial consultation to discuss your project requirements, goals, and how we can help you achieve them. This helps us understand your needs and provide you with a tailored proposal.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have experience working with clients across various industries including e-commerce, retail, hospitality, tourism, technology, healthcare, education, and professional services. Our strategies are tailored to each industry's unique challenges and opportunities.

How do you measure the success of digital marketing campaigns?

We use a variety of metrics depending on your goals, including website traffic, conversion rates, lead generation, ROI, engagement rates, and brand awareness. We provide monthly reports with detailed analytics and insights to track progress and optimize performance.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is customized based on the scope of work, project complexity, and duration. We offer both project-based pricing for one-time projects and monthly retainers for ongoing services. During our consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed proposal outlining costs and deliverables.
